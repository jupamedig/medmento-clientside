What we can do for you

Medications you have been prescribed are more likely to be effective if you follow your doctor’s or pharmacist’s instructions on when to take them. Many times we forget to take our medication or worry about a friend or family member forgetting to take their medication.We solve this issue with a simple phone call, reminding patients when it's time take their medication. Rest assured - our team has you and your loved ones covered!

Step 1

To get up-and-running, create a profile to get started.

Step 2

Pick a date and time to schedule your phone call reminder.

Step 3

A phone call will be sent to the patient when it's time to take their medication.
